Way of Thinking:
    - Be Prepared: 
        Know yourself / companies
    - Critical Thinking: 
        Motivation analysis from other people’s perspective
        Pros / Cons
        Consequence
        Do not be followers (copy or influenced by others’ opinions)
    - Systematic Way of Thinking: 
        Initiatives (overview, complexities)
        Road maps (details)
        Dependencies (ordering)
        Executions (verifications)
        Results (failure / risk management)

Way of Talking:
    - Do not confront your interviewers
    - How to talk (unless you know exactly what you are saying):
        When having nothing nice to say, you choose not to say it. 
        Never criticize your previous employers (not too much).
        Calm and reasonable

BQ 1: Your weaknesses? 
    - 不要王婆卖瓜自卖自夸，诚实！！！！！！
    - How to improve yourself in the future?

*BQ 2: When you have different opinions from your colleagues?
    - Scenarios -- 要给出具体的实例
    - Communication (How, When, Where) – Keep in mind: Say something for a reason, how to praise someone, how to criticize someone
    - Take Actions: Knowledge sharing, provide concrete data
    - What if? – Back up solutions
    - 如果你就是知道你上级做的决定是错误的怎么办？
		分类讨论：如果是小问题，可以先跟着上级的思路去做
				如果是大问题，一定要promote more communication/向更上级汇报（谨慎，有些公司不能跨级汇报）

*BQ 3: When you have limited time available to finish your project? Quality vs speed?
    - We need to sit down with all parties to analyze which feature is the most important (feature prioritization). 
    - Agile Development

*BQ 4: Most challenging project?
    - It is an important question to test your limit.
    - Why is it challenging? Technical, human resource, time?
    - 千万不可以把project内容说的很冗长，只分享high-level idea
    - How did you solve it?
    - How did you evaluate the result? Scope & Impact & What did you learn

BQ 5: Tell me one failure in the past.
    - More critical is not just to tell the story
    - Any rememdy work?
	- What you learned? How can you improve in the future?

*BQ 6: Describe a stressful situation at work and how you handled it?
    - 主动的Stress：能力/经验不够，现实残酷
	- 被动的Stress：上级给你的，peer pressure
    - Make plans, try to get very well-prepared for future challenges
    - Look for helps from others (更有经验的老员工，心理医生)

BQ 7: What will you do if you were given a very vague task?
    - Gather opinion / details
    - Make a plan
    - Have a prototype or what we call MVP (most viable product);
    - Build upon that

BQ 8: What is your career plan for the next 5 years?
    - From where you are to where you want to be. HOW???????

BQ 9: Do you have any questions for me?
    - Test how eager you are to join the company.
    - DO NOT DIRECTLY ask about work-life balance and privacy questions:
        - Could you describe a typical work day as a software engineer at XXX?
    - Tech-oriented 你们组用的是什么技术呀？你们现在主要找什么方面能力比较强的人呀？
        - If I had the privilege to join your team, what is your suggestion before I start my internship/work?
        - Could you please tell me what project or problems are you currently working on?
        - Could you please tell me what have you learned so far from working in your current team?
